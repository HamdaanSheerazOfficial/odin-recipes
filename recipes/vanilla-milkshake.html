Vanilla Milkshake

Craving something creamy and sweet? This Vanilla Milkshake is the perfect treat!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Place 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream in a blender
  2. Add 200ml of milk and a splash of vanilla extract
  3. Blend until smooth, and add sugar to taste
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
