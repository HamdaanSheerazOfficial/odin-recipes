Strawberry Smoothie

Refreshing, healthy, and perfect for a summer morning! Try this Strawberry Smoothie.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Yogurt
  • Honey or Sugar
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place 1 cup of fresh strawberries in a blender
  2. Add 1/2 cup of yogurt and a spoonful of honey or sugar
  3. Add a handful of ice cubes
  4. Blend until smooth and pour into a glass
  5. Enjoy your refreshing smoothie!
